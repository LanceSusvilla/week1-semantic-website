Red Stewed Chicken

Price

₱180

About this Dish

Tender chicken slow-cooked in a rich, savory red stew made with aromatic herbs and flavorful spices. Every bite is juicy, comforting, and packed with homemade goodness—perfectly served with steamed rice.

Ingredients

  • Chicken (thighs, drumsticks, or breast)
  • Tomato sauce or crushed tomatoes
  • Tomato paste
  • Black Pepper
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Bell peppers
  • Carrots (optional)
  • Cooking oil
  • Fresh parsley or green onions (for garnish)
  • Brown sugar (optional)
  • Salt
